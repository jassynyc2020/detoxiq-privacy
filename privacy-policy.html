Detox IQ — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Detox IQ ("we", "our", or "the app") is an educational wellness application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Detox IQ does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require account creation, login, or registration of any kind.

2. AI Chatbot

The AI chatbot feature is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. When you send a message to the chatbot, your message is transmitted to Anthropic's servers to generate a response. Detox IQ does not store any chatbot conversations. Please refer to Anthropic's Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy for information on how they handle data.

3. Audio Player

The Sound Healing audio player generates tones locally on your device using the Web Audio API. No audio data is transmitted or stored.

4. Analytics

Detox IQ does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your usage of the app in any way.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

Anthropic Claude API — Powers the AI chatbot. Messages sent to the chatbot are processed by Anthropic. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

Detox IQ is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 18.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
